HR for non HR Managers

All business decisions involve human resources. Supervisors must handle human capital, and even technically skilled managers require basic HR knowledge. Effectively managing human resources can unlock significant value.

The course is designed for those in supervisory positions and managers desirous of attracting best talent in their departments and drive superior performance

HR Data Analytics

Analyzing HR trends aids in predicting changes for refreshed talent and organizational resilience, using metrics for predictive analytics, enhancing engagement, and strategic HR roles.

The course is designed for middle and senior level HR officers/managers in human resources management and development, recruitment & selection committee members, public service commissions.

Effective report and minutes writing

Effective communication sustains teamwork, clarity, and accountability. Writing concise reports for diverse audiences is a valuable skill. This course focuses on report writing techniques for decision-making.

The course is designed for managers and any other officers who are expected to write and present various reports or act as secretaries in meetings.

Talent Development

Organizations face talent development challenges due to budget constraints and uneven distribution. Prioritizing long-term investment aligns talent pipelines with business goals. Post-development, strategic alignment ensures added value.

The course is designed for senior HR/HC or Knowledge Management professionals and managers within public or private sector organizations responsible for managing and developing employees.

Executive Assistant of the future

Executive assistants historically managed offices and schedules adeptly. Despite workplace evolution, many remain constrained by traditional norms. As work shifts to hybrid, tech-driven spaces, assistants must adapt to new roles influenced by technology and interconnected structures.

The course is designed for executive secretaries, office administrators, PAs, senior secretaries, executive assistants and administrative executives.

LEADERSHIP

Leadership Development Programme

In any setting, leadership is crucial for success, guiding change amid turbulence to enhance organizational resilience and agility. The evolving hybrid, tech-driven era demands transformational, results-oriented leadership, posing questions on past training gaps and necessitating a new toolbox for navigating the 'new normal.'

The course is designed for Governors, Deputy Governors, Principal Secretaries, CEOs, Directors, Deputy Directors, Commissioners and other Senior Managers in public, private sector and NGOs.

Leadership for Results

Leaders and managers should excel in three areas: personal performance, departmental success, and forecasting future trends. They must prioritize value over processes, ensuring results are clear, measurable, and celebrated. Reevaluating how we define and measure results, adjusting leadership approaches, and strategizing next steps upon achievement are crucial for success.

This course will be beneficial to senior and middle level managers in both private and public institutions.

Leading Change in a Volatile Environment

Conventional approaches falter as known norms are challenged, and resilience becomes paramount. Adaptation reigns supreme across all sectors. Organizations must reassess their readiness to navigate the multidimensional and dynamic nature of change, bracing themselves for unprecedented challenges ahead.

The course is designed for Principal Secretaries, CEOs, Directors, Deputy Directors, Chairpersons of Boards and other Senior Officers

Transformative Leadership

Transformative leadership prioritizes vision, impact, and people. It focuses on learning, trust-building, and exploring new pathways. Leaders inspire mindset shifts, remain optimistic, and credit others. However, such leaders are increasingly rare. Leaders must challenge the status quo and embrace transformation. This course will challenge participants' thinking and apply transformational leadership tools.

The course is designed for Senior Managers in organizations and those tasked to lead major transformation efforts. Those tasked in design of new services and products (Product and Service Development Managers), Business Process experts and Managers involved in change implementation.

Leadership in Public Institutions

Evidence shows leadership quality is declining, seen in eroding trust and integrity. Public sector leaders must navigate complexities while upholding leadership principles. The scarcity of good leaders raises questions about their rarity. Public institution leaders must innovate, negotiate, and provide direction amidst challenges. Maintaining leadership virtues is crucial while driving reform and collaborative efforts.

The course is designed for Governors, Deputy Governors, Principal Secretaries, Members of County Public Service Boards, Directors, Commissioners, Assistant Commissioners, Chief Executive Officers and Senior Managers in public enterprises, Executive agencies and local government Institutions.
